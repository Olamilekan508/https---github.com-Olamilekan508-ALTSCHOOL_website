Akon_-_Cant_Say_No

Akon, born Aliaune Thiam on April 16, 1973, in St. Louis, Missouri, is an American-Senegalese singer, songwriter, record producer, and entrepreneur. His father, Mor Thiam, is a well-known jazz percussionist from Senegal, which exposed Akon to music from an early age. His mother, who is of Mauritanian descent, also played a significant role in his upbringing.
